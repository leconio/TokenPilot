# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project. Be constructive, assume good intent while addressing concrete impact, respect privacy, and focus criticism on the work rather than the person.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained disruption. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when needed to protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers.
